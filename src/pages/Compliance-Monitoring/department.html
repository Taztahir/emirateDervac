Compliance Monitoring Department

The Compliance Monitoring Engineering (CME) Department primarily exists to independently monitor all of the Emirates continuing airworthiness management functions, aircraft maintenance and engineering
support activities to ensure they remain in compliance with the published engineering procedures,
customer requirements, industry standards, as well as the various National/International Civil Aviation
regulations.

Compliance monitoring is conducted by a dedicated team of trained and experienced quality auditors to objectively assess the effectiveness of the organisation’s policies and procedures providing necessary confidence that all corporate and regulatory requirements are met.
