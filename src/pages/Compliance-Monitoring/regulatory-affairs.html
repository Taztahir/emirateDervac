Compliance Monitoring Regulatory Affairs

The Compliance Monitoring Engineering â€“ Regulatory Affairs (CME-RA) section is responsible for the
acquisition, timely renewal, amendment and/or extension of all organisation approvals necessary for
Emirates Engineering to deliver its premium aircraft engineering products and associated technical services
to Emirates aircraft fleet and its global third party operators and customers. The section also responsible
for the development, management and publication of regulatory expositions (ie. CAME, MOE and
Supplements) as well as the administration, evaluation and control of departmental manuals, engineering
procedures, forms and quality notices.

The Regulatory Affairs section also liaise and serve as
a touch point of Emirates business stakeholders,

requirements, changes in industry regulations or

In addition to its core oversight functions, the CME-RA roles critically support the Emirates engineering
business in terms of:

Regulatory Approvals: Securing the necessary approvals, authorisation and/or permits from local/foreign
national aviation authorities is the key to unlock the world-class aircraft engineering services and
component capabilities to support the continuing airworthiness of Emirates aircraft fleet, external
customer aircraft and components requirements. Some of these sustained approvals include:

  • UAE General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) CAR M (Continuing Airworthiness Management)
  • UAE General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) CAR 145 (Maintenance)
  • UAE General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) CAR 147 (Training)
  • UAE General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) CAR 21 (Design)
  • UAE General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) CAR MOA (Manufacturing)
  • European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA) Part 145 (Maintenance)
  • European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA) Part 147 (Training)
  • European Union Aviation Safety Agency (EASA) Part 21 (Design)
  • Other NAA Part 145 Maintenance Organisation Approvals from CAA United Kingdom, CAA Singapore,
    CAA Thailand, CAA Egypt, CAA Qatar, Kuwait DGCA, Bermuda CAA and Korea MOLIT.
  • ISO 17025:2017 Testing and Calibration Laboratories
