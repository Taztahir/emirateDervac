Engineering Materials Management

This department is responsible for developing policies, processes and
procedures to support operations with optimised inventory investment and
operating costs.

This includes forecasting, planning, stock reviews, replenishment and
Outstations & AOG Material support.

The Material Management department, is a team of professionals with extensive knowledge and
experience of complex issues within the field. The team is organised along commodity lines; Cabin, IFE,
Airframe, Engines, Inventory Control and Operations Support, as well as a dedicated team managing
Rotable Components and Outstation support.

The section's prime responsibility is to ensure timely availability of spares and components to support
material demands at main base and across the network in most cost effective manner. This is carried out
by establishing optimum re-order and safety levels, evaluating & forecasting requirements, initiating
purchase requests and co-ordinating with Procurement department for timely deliveries of spares on
Purchase & Repairs Orders. The department is also responsible for the management & control of entire
inventory, including its data accuracy, stock verification and surplus evaluations follows the highest
standards of quality and safety policies, as defined by aviation agencies such as GCAA, ATA, FAA, EASA. One
of its core functions is to provide 24X7 support for Critical/AOG requirements of all aircraft maintenance
spares activities & operations. The team also arranges operations support at outstations through Pooling
Loans and Borrowing.

A spares supply service and AOG support is provided 24 hours a day in Dubai, as well as at all Emirates
outstations. Being a member of the IATP, spares and equipment are pooled at several stations to support
Emirates and other airlines.
